How It Works

From surplus to impact in five steps

Our AI handles the heavy lifting — quality checks, matching, and logistics — so good food moves fast.

Step 1

Donate

List surplus food in under 2 minutes with photos, quantity, and pickup window.

Step 2

AI Analysis

Our model checks freshness, quantity, and safe consumption windows automatically.

Step 3

Smart Matching

The nearest NGO with matching need and capacity is notified instantly.

Step 4

Pickup

A volunteer or partner driver collects and delivers food on an optimized route.

Step 5

Impact Tracking

Donors get a receipt with meals saved, CO₂ avoided, and the NGO they helped.
